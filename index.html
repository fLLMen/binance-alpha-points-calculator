How Points Are Calculated (Assumed Model for Planning)

Your total points are the sum of Balance Points and Volume Points over the last 15 days.

  • Balance Points: Earned daily based on your asset balance tier. This calculator assumes your balance tier is constant for the full 15-day window. Total assumed balance points = daily balance points * 15 days.
  • Volume Points: Earned daily based on your *daily* Alpha token purchase volume. The points for a given day (V_daily) are floor(logâ‚‚(V_daily)) for V_daily >= $2, and 0 for V_daily < $2. Total volume points = sum of daily volume points over the 15-day window.

"Double Volume" Promotion: If applied, daily purchase volume that is from BSC Alpha token purchases OR Limit buy orders is effectively double-counted towards the *daily Alpha Volume* used for point calculation. To minimize actual traded volume, this calculator assumes you use *only* eligible trade types (BSC purchases or Limit buys) during the promotion period.

This calculator helps you figure out the required *minimum daily actual purchase volume* needed (using eligible trades under the promo, if applied) to cover the points your *assumed 15-day balance points* don't provide, planning this volume over your specified number of trading days within that window.

This calculator assumes you will maintain the selected balance tier *consistently* for the entire 15-day rolling window used by Binance. It calculates the total points you would get from this balance over 15 days. It then figures out how many additional volume points are needed and provides a daily purchase volume plan over your specified trading days to earn those volume points with minimal volume. If the promotion is applied, the daily required volume calculation assumes you are performing *only eligible trades* (BSC purchases or Limit buys). This plan assumes trading occurs on consecutive days starting from "Day 1". You only need to perform the *purchase* volume; selling does not contribute to points. Trading fees are not included.
